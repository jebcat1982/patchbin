About

Patchbin is a better way to send patches to fellow developers on Mailing lists, IRC etc, and get feedback and reviews. Paste a patch in the GNU Diff format, and hit "Submit" to get a shortened link to your patch.

To post a comment, double click on the line, and use the form to post your comment. You can also reply to existing comments.

I built this site as a learning exercise in Django. If you find something that needs changing or fixing, please email me at ("anirudh@" + "anirudhs" + "anjeev.org"), I'm still very new to all this. The code is also on github
